Mediation in a money claims dispute

Mediation is only available if you:

  • are willing to negotiate on the amount of the claim and will consider a compromise
  • have enough information about the claim to enter into negotiations, and do not require any further evidence from the other party before starting mediation
  • are available for the entire time slot on the day of your appointment

If you cannot answer yes to all 3, mediation is not suitable for your case and you should contact us.
